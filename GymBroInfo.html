üîß How to Use

Setting Up Measurements

Navigate to the Measurements section to log your current height, weight, and other body metrics. GymBro will automatically track changes over time and display your progress in easy-to-read charts.

Creating Workout Templates

Build your ideal workout routine by creating templates for different training days. Whether you follow a Push/Pull/Legs split or prefer Upper/Lower body days, customize your templates to match your training style.

Example Push Day Template:

  • Bench Press - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Shoulder Press - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Dips - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Lateral Raises - 3 sets x 15 reps

Logging Workouts

During your workout, use your template to log sets, reps, and weights. The app saves your progress automatically and helps you track improvements session by session.

Managing Exercises

Add new exercises to your library or remove ones you don't use. Each exercise can include custom notes, target muscles, and instructions.
