Privacy Policy for GymBro

Effective Date: May 25th, 2025

Overview

GymBro is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our gym activity tracking application handles your information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

GymBro is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to our servers or any third parties. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Exercise data and workout logs
  • Weight and body measurement data
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

Local Data Storage

All data you enter into GymBro, including:

  • Exercise routines and workout logs
  • Weight tracking information
  • Body measurements
  • Session history
  • App preferences and settings

is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device secure with appropriate lock screen protection
  • Regularly backing up your device if you want to preserve your GymBro data
  • Being aware that uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your local data

Feedback and Support

GymBro includes a feature that allows you to contact us via email for feedback or support. If you choose to email us:

  • We will only use your email address to respond to your inquiry
  • We do not store your email address in any database or mailing list
  • We do not share your contact information with third parties
  • Any information you voluntarily include in your email is used solely for support purposes

Third-Party Services

GymBro does not integrate with or use any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs that would collect or process your data.

Children's Privacy

GymBro does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect any personal information from any users, this policy applies to users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and will take effect immediately upon posting. Your continued use of GymBro after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete individual entries within the app
  • You can clear all data through the app's settings
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all associated data

Your Rights

Since GymBro does not collect or store any of your personal information on our servers:

  • There is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete
  • All data control and management occurs directly on your device
  • You maintain complete ownership and control of your information at all times

This Privacy Policy demonstrates our commitment to protecting your privacy by design - we simply don't collect what we don't need.
